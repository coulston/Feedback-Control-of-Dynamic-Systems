A hydraulic system is used in an airplane to move the control surfaces such as the ailerons and rudder. In flight, the control surfaces can be modeled as mass, along with a damper to model friction, and a spring which models the force due to the air passing over the control surface. The linearized servo valve equation is $\delta q = k_{x} \delta x - k_{p} \delta p$. Find the transfer function $\delta Y(s)/\delta X(s)$.
